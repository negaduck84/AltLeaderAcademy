Our Development Programs

Our leadership development programs are designed to help grow the skills and capabilities of our people to effectively deliver on our leadership principles, which are the key characteristics that make our leadership team great --- leading by our values, genuinely caring for our people, building trust, communicating effectively and delivering results.

Developed to be a Journey

  • Supervisor / Manager
  • Professional
  • Senior Leader

This program is designed to help supervisors and managers be the best they can be and deliver on our Leadership Principles as the foundation of their success. The program has six sessions that begin with helping new leaders orient to our leadership culture of care with the remaining sessions focused on building the knowledge, skills and capabilities in leading yourself and others, effectively communicating, owning your business & executing for outcomes, leading inclusively and leading change.

Click here for a copy of the journey map.

IN DEVELOPMENT

This program is designed to help professionals build foundational skills, deliver on our Leadership Principles, and support them in owning and building their career. Whether it’s to deepen their experience or grow into a leadership path. The program is currently under development but will focus on building the knowledge, skills and capabilities in areas such as cultural awareness and inclusion, business comprehension, leading self, building effective relationships, influence and accountability, agility and adaptability, etc. More to come!

IN DEVELOPMENT

This program is designed to provide senior leaders continuous learning and development opportunities to help them reach their potential and effectively deliver on the Leadership Principles. The program is currently under development but will focus on building the knowledge, skills and capabilities in areas such as strategic planning, high-performance team development, change leadership, management systems (designing & improving outcomes), ownership mentality, etc.. More to come!

Welcome to our Leadership Team

Understand our leadership culture, who you are as a leader, the importance of building trusted & inclusive relationships, and the support & resources available to you.

  • Leading with care
  • Building trusting relationships
  • Know and understand your resources

This session is in-person and is 6 hours, designed to be completed within 30 days of hire or promotion.

Effective Communication

Learn how to align, engage, inspire, unite and mobilize your team through effective communication.

  • Feedback vs. coaching
  • Sense making & meaning making
  • Difficult conversations
  • Managing conflict

Ownership & Execution

Build the skills to demonstrate your ownership and deliver results.

  • Problem solving & critical thinking
  • Intro to project management (for leaders)
  • Decision making
  • Intro to management systems

Leadership Essentials 1 [Harvard]

Analyze, discuss and practice the skills required to build your self-awareness and a high-performing team in this multi-week Harvard development program.

  • Leading yourself (growth mindset, agility, resilience, emotional intelligence)
  • Leading others (delegation, High Performance Teams, Influence)

This is an external session, facilitated by Harvard, is virtual, and lasts (7) weeks.

Inclusive Leadership

Learn how to create a safe environment, interrupt bias, advocate and intervene to ensure equity & inclusion, and help your team and those around you grow their inclusive skills.

  • Leading with equity and inclusion
  • Psychological safety, trust, and relationships
  • Mitigating bias

Leadership Essentials 2

Learn how to shift to a longer term view of your business, influence and shape the strategy going forward, and lead the change that results from executing the strategy.

  • Leading change
  • Influencing strategies
  • Big picture thinking

Frequently Asked Questions

The Leader Academy provides learning opportunities to employees reach their potential with access to in-depth resources and self-service e-learnings on growing yourself and growing others.

There are multiple ways to participate.
  1. If you are a new supervisor or manager, you will be assigned the supervisor/manager leadership development program to register for all (6) learning sessions.
  2. You have a conversation with your leader, develop a plan, and register for the learning session to support you.
  3. Search the learning library and take a self-serve course.

Currently there is one (1) program; Supervisor / Manager development program, however, there are programs in development for all roles.
